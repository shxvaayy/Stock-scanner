The Three Strategies

All three run simultaneously during market hours, each with its own capital and hard loss caps.

1 · RSI Bounce

5-min mean reversion · 10:15 AM – 2:00 PM

Waits for a Nifty-50 stock to get panic-sold (5-min RSI below 20), confirms the bounce has started, then buys the recovery. Six filters must all pass — uptrend intact, above VWAP, 1.5× volume, real momentum.

Exit: half at RSI 50, rest on full recovery · ATR stop · 75-min time stop
Max 3 positions · daily loss cap ₹7,500

2 · RSI Multi-Timeframe

15-min + 5-min alignment · 10:15 AM – 2:30 PM

Same bounce idea but three screens must agree: the daily chart allows mean reversion, the 15-min chart is oversold in a choppy market (low KER), and the 5-min chart prints a green trigger candle with weak-buyer volume (MFI < 30).

Exit: RSI 50 / VWAP touch / 75 min · 3×ATR disaster stop · hard close 2:30 PM
Max 3 positions · daily loss cap ₹7,500

3 · Iron Condor

Nifty options · weekly expiry day only

On expiry day, sells a put + call around Nifty's price and buys protective wings. If Nifty stays inside the range till close, the sold options decay to zero and the premium is kept. Max loss is capped by the wings.

Runs once a week · VIX-gated (no trade above VIX 22)
Blackout on RBI / Budget / Fed days
